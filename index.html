About DREAMRS Lab

The Design Research and Human Factors (DREAMRS) Lab works in the two core areas of design – 'Design Research' and 'Human Factors'. Within design research, the lab focuses on design creativity, design methods, and tools, biologically inspired design, knowledge-based systems, and design processes in resource- constrained environments, and allied areas. Within Human Factors, the current projects focus on developing ergonomic solutions in the field of technical products.

DREAMRS lab is associated with Human Centred Design at IIIT Delhi. This lab is founded and led by Dr. Sonal Keshwani (Ph.D., IISc Bangalore).

Within DREAMRS Lab

Design Research

We focus on design creativity, methods and tools, biologically inspired design, knowledge based systems, design processes in resource constrained environments. We aim to improve the ways products, services, and systems are conceptualized and developed, ensuring meaningful advancements in the field of design.

  • • Biologically Inspired Design
  • • Knowledge-Based Systems
  • • Resource-Constrained Design Processes

Human Factors

We study human behavior, capabilities, and limitations to ensure that our designs align with user needs. By integrating product and cognitive ergonomics, our research addresses critical aspects of safety, performance, and well-being, delivering optimized systems and products for real-world applications.

  • • Product Ergonomics
  • • Safety and Performance Optimization
  • • User-Centered Design
